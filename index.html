About Sokoine College

We are dedicated to providing high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers in today's competitive job market.

Your Path to Success Begins Here

Founded in 2005, Sokoine College has established itself as a premier institution of higher learning in Tanzania. Located in the beautiful area of Kaloleni in Arusha, our college offers a conducive environment for learning and personal growth.

Our dedicated team of experienced educators and staff are committed to providing students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to excel in their chosen careers.

Admission Information

We welcome applications from qualified candidates who are passionate about learning and personal growth.

Entry Requirements

  • Completion of Form IV with a minimum of 4 passes for Certificate programs
  • Completion of Form VI or equivalent for Diploma programs
  • Prior diploma in relevant field for Advanced Diploma programs
  • Basic computer literacy for all IT programs
  • English language proficiency
Learn More

Application Process

  • Complete the online application form
  • Submit required documents (transcripts, certificates, ID)
  • Pay application fee (non-refundable)
  • Attend interview (if required)
  • Receive admission letter

Join Our College

Sokoine College offers admissions twice a year in March and September. We welcome applications from qualified students who are passionate about building a successful career.

Requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Certified copies of academic certificates
  • Copy of identification document
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Application fee payment receipt

Important Dates:

March Intake: Applications open from December to February

September Intake: Applications open from June to August

Our Facilities

We provide modern facilities to ensure a comfortable and productive learning environment.

Computer Laboratories

State-of-the-art computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and software to support hands-on learning and practical training in information technology.

Library & Resource Center

A well-stocked library with textbooks, reference materials, journals, and digital resources to support research and academic excellence.

Training Kitchen & Restaurant
